EduMental

"For Students By Students"

Many people today tend to ignore the present mental health issues among students in New York City. In 2019, when the COVID-19 pandemic started, depression among teens from the ages of 15-19 spiked by an overwhelming 35.9%. Over the past year with online school and Zoom classes, one can only imagine how much this percentage has increased. Our mission is to create a way for students to easily be able to share their mental health experiences.
