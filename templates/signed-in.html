Mental Health

This website is for spreading awareness on Mental Heath and helping students who have issues to get help.

"For Students By Students"

Mental health is a challenge for many people especially teenagers in middle and high school. With the Covid pandemic this issue has been exacerbated and yet there are very few website specifically oriented towards students. This is why we have made a website for students to share their experiences and use resources to help better their mental health.
